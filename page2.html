Federico Citterich Portfolio

Federico Citterich is a Rome-based science journalist and communicator who focuses on climate change, sustainability, and biodiversity. He currently works as a TV journalist at Canale 21 and as a science communicator at FLIM LABS. He also collaborates with the Institute of Polar Sciences of the Italian National Research Council, with which he took part in two research expeditions to the North Pole (September 2024 and July 2025). In addition, he contributes as a freelance science journalist to various Italian and international outlets, including L'Osservatore Romano, Vatican News, Onyx, and Newsweek.
